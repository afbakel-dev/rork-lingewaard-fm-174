# Build a Lingewaard Radio streaming app

I'll build a beautiful mobile app for Lingewaard Radio — a local Dutch radio station — with live streaming, now-playing info, and a polished player experience.

**Features**
- Tap a big play button to listen to the live Lingewaard Radio stream
- See the current track, artist, and station artwork while listening
- Background audio playback with lock-screen controls
- Schedule view showing the weekly program lineup
- News & updates feed from the station
- Favorite tracks list — save songs you discover while listening
- Volume control and a sleep timer

**Design**
- Warm, broadcast-inspired aesthetic with deep midnight blue and a vibrant orange-red accent (echoing radio tower / on-air vibes)
- Animated sound-wave visualizer that pulses while audio is playing
- Glassy, layered cards with soft shadows and rounded corners
- Subtle "ON AIR" indicator with a gentle blinking dot
- Smooth spring animations between screens and on the play button
- Custom typography pairing a bold display font with a clean sans-serif for body
- Haptic feedback on key interactions

**Screens**
- **Live**: Hero player with album art, now-playing info, big play/pause, waveform visualizer, sleep timer, and favorite button
- **Schedule**: Weekly program grid with show hosts and times, current show highlighted
- **News**: Scrollable feed of station news and announcements
- **Favorites**: List of tracks you've saved while listening, with timestamp

**App icon**
- Bold orange-red radio tower silhouette emitting waves on a deep midnight blue gradient background
